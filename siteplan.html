Anambra Jobs & Skills Hub – Site Plan

Project blueprint for an informational three-page website

1. Site Name

AnambraJobsHub.ng

Short, memorable, and instantly tells users the site is a one-stop hub for employment and skills opportunities within Anambra State.

2. Site Purpose

To connect youths and job-seekers in Anambra State with verified local job openings, vocational training, apprenticeships, and career-development resources while offering employers and trainers a trusted platform to broadcast opportunities.

3. Scenarios (Target-Visitor Questions)

  1. “I just finished NYSC in Awka—are there any entry-level jobs near me that aren’t scams?”
  2. “I want to learn solar-panel installation; which accredited centres in Anambra offer short courses and maybe a stipend?”
